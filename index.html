Data-Centric IoT-Cloud Service Platform for Smart Communities

Empowering TEIN communities with centralized IoT storage, analytics, and gateway services across Thailand and Laos

Explore Platform

Supported By

A collaborative initiative involving Thailand, Laos, Malaysia, Japan, and Korea

Key Platform Capabilities

Centralized IoT Storage

Secure, scalable data storage infrastructure hosted in Thailand's Data Lake, accessible to all TEIN communities for IoT data management and archival.

Analytics Compute Pool

Powerful distributed computing resources enabling real-time data processing, machine learning, and advanced analytics for smart community applications.

IoT Access Gateway

Unified API gateway providing seamless connectivity and integration capabilities for diverse IoT devices and applications across the TEIN network.

Demonstration Sites

Smart-Energy/Mobility@Chula

Chulalongkorn University, Thailand

Comprehensive smart energy management and mobility solutions showcasing IoT-enabled campus optimization, energy efficiency monitoring, and sustainable transportation systems.

Smart-Agriculture@NUOL

National University of Laos, Laos

Advanced agricultural IoT system featuring greenhouse monitoring, crop optimization, and precision farming techniques. Winner of best poster presentation at AINTEC/CFI 2019.
